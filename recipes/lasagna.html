lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound ground beef or sausage
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the meat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
