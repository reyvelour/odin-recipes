Taco Skillet Recipe

This Taco Skillet is a quick and easy one-pan meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. It's packed with flavor and can be customized with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Shredded cheese for topping
  • Sour cream and chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or turkey over medium heat.
  2. Drain excess fat and add taco seasoning, black beans, corn, and salsa.
  3. Stir to combine and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Top with shredded cheese and cover until melted.
  6. Serve with sour cream and chopped cilantro on top.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Taco Skillet!
