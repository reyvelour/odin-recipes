Korean Beef Bowl Recipe

This Korean Beef Bowl is a quick and easy dish that combines ground beef with a savory sauce, served over rice. It's perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Cooked rice for serving
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil to the skillet.
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve over cooked rice and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Korean Beef Bowl!
